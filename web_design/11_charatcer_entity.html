HTML Character Entities Demo

HTML character entities are special codes used to represent characters in HTML that either cannot be included directly or have special meaning in HTML. They are used to ensure proper rendering of text and to avoid conflicts with HTML syntax.

Character Entity Description
© &copy; Copyright symbol
< &lt; Less than
> &gt; Greater than
& &amp; Ampersand
" &quot; Double quotation mark
' &apos; Apostrophe (single quote)
© &#169; Copyright symbol (numeric reference)
> &#62; Greater than (numeric reference)
< &#60; Less than (numeric reference)
